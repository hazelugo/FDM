\#\# Section 1: Strategic Vision  
\*Knowing your high-level goals ensures the design serves your bottom line.\*

We are building this website for a church that is trying to reach a wider audience and begin working in the community. We want to be a place where people can come to feel welcome and to feel helped.  
Three things I want this website to achieve are to increase community awareness, boost attendance, and showcase the ministries the organization offers.  
Two other local churches have really well designed websites that showcase all of their programs and have a lot of attractive media assets.  
If this website increases growth by even 5% in 6 months, this project will be a huge win.  
\---

\#\# Section 2: Audience Psychology  
\*Websites should be built for the person using them, not just the person owning them.\*

My target age is young adults (18-40) who are comfortable with technology but also older people might visit. People who are only familiar with social media.  
I am not sure if there are any pain points but maybe having questions about programs/schedules we offer.  
If there is only one thing that a visitor to my site does, is make the decision to join us for Sunday service.  
A user should feel a sense of love, joy, hope, uplifted, inspired when my homepage loads.

\---

\#\# Section 3: Creative Direction  
\*Visual identity communicates authority and personality without saying a word.\*

Five adjectives to describe this brand are reliable, calm, loving, traditional, inspiring.  
Use colors that inspire calm and hope, do not use imagery that makes the website or the organization appear too outdated. Traditional is good but outdated is not good.

\---

\#\# Section 4: Content & Structure  
\*Content is the engine that drives your search engine rankings and user trust.\*

Essential pages are: Home, About, Schedule, Programs, Contact)  
You can use placeholders for text and graphics  
The tone/writing should be welcoming and friendly, demonstrate casual but not overly casual, this is still a serious organization that wants to serve the community.
