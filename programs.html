Programs & Ministries

Get involved.
Make a difference.

Our ministries are how faith gets put into action. Find a program that speaks to your season of life and come grow alongside us.

We believe that everyone has something to offer and something to receive. Whether you're looking to serve, to grow, or simply to belong — there's a place for you here. Below you'll find the ministries that make up the heartbeat of our church.

🤝
01
Ministry

Community Outreach

Our outreach ministry is the hands and feet of Fountain of Mercy in the broader community. Through food drives, neighborhood clean-ups, partnerships with local shelters, and seasonal events, we work to show that love is more than a word.

No experience is needed — just a willing heart. We welcome volunteers at all levels of involvement, from one-time events to regular weekly service.

When Saturdays, schedule varies
Who All ages, families welcome
Contact outreach@fountainofmercy.org

02
Ministry

Worship & Arts

Worship is central to who we are. Our Worship and Arts ministry includes our praise band, choir, visual arts team, and creative media volunteers who help bring each Sunday service to life.

If you have a gift for music, art, audio/visual, or creative production, we'd love to have you on the team.

Rehearsals Saturdays 10:00 AM
Who Teens & adults with music/creative interest
Contact worship@fountainofmercy.org
Join the Team

03
Ministry

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry (grades 6–12) is a vibrant community where teenagers can have fun, ask real questions, and discover who they are in faith. Friday Youth Group meets every week with worship, games, and honest conversation.

We also plan seasonal retreats, service projects, and community events throughout the year.

When Fridays, 7:00 PM
Who Ages 12–18 (grades 6–12)
Contact youth@fountainofmercy.org
Learn More
👶
04
Ministry

Children's Church

We are passionate about giving children a solid foundation in faith from the very beginning. Our children's ministry offers engaging, age-appropriate programming every Sunday during both services — from the nursery all the way through 5th grade.

Our rooms are safe, staffed by background-checked volunteers, and designed to make kids excited about coming to church.

When Sundays, both services
Who Infants through 5th grade
Contact children@fountainofmercy.org
