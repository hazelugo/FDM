Our Story

Rooted here.
Growing together.

Fountain of Mercy was founded in 2004 by a small group of families with a simple vision: to create a church where anyone could walk through the doors and feel at home. What started with 40 people in a rented school gymnasium has grown into a congregation of hundreds, united by a shared love for God and neighbor.

Over the years, we've grown our outreach programs, expanded our youth and children's ministries, and deepened our roots in the surrounding community. We've seen lives changed — not through grand gestures, but through consistent presence and genuine care.

We are traditional in our faith and forward-looking in our approach. That means timeless truths delivered in ways that connect with people wherever they are. We don't believe faith has to be stuffy or inaccessible — it should be real, relatable, and life-giving.

What We Believe

The things that guide
everything we do.

01

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the foundation for how we live, love, and serve.

02

Belonging

Everyone deserves a church family. We are committed to making sure no one feels unseen or unwelcome here.

03

Service

Faith without action isn't enough. We serve our community because love is something you do, not just something you feel.

04

Growth

We're all works in progress. We create space for honest questions, deeper discipleship, and genuine transformation.

05

Worship

Worship is more than music — it's a posture of the heart. We pursue meaningful, Spirit-led encounters every service.

06

Community

Life is better together. We encourage real, authentic relationships built on trust, prayer, and showing up for each other.
