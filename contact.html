Common Questions

Quick answers.

What should I wear?

Come as you are — literally. We have no dress code. Some people wear jeans, some wear their Sunday best. You'll fit right in either way.

Is there childcare?

Yes! We offer nursery care and children's programming during both Sunday services. Check in your little ones when you arrive.

How long are services?

Both Sunday services run approximately 75–90 minutes, including worship music and a message from our pastor.

Do I need to register?

Nope — just show up. If you'd like to let us know you're coming so we can personally welcome you, you're welcome to reach out first.

Can I watch online?

Yes! We stream our 11 AM Sunday service live on Facebook and YouTube. Links are on our social media pages.

Is parking available?

We have a free parking lot adjacent to the church building, plus street parking nearby. Accessible spaces are near the entrance.

Still have questions?

message away.
